## How To Present It

Do not treat the presentation and the system as two separate things.

Do it like this:

- speak on each intro screen while it is visible
- click `Next`
- move to the next exact message
- when you reach `The full product is running right now.`
- click `Enter app`
- continue speaking inside the live system

## Exact Screen-By-Screen Script

### Screen 1

Visible text:

- `01 · The Problem`
- `Institutions release money before they can verify who should receive it.`

Say:

`This is the problem we are solving.`

`Many institutions still move money before they are fully sure who should receive it.`

`That creates financial loss for the institution and delay for the real beneficiary.`

Click:

- `Next`

### Screen 2

Visible text:

- `02 · Who It Serves`
- `Two groups are caught in the middle.`

Say:

`Two groups are affected.`

`The institution needs confidence before approving payment.`

`The beneficiary needs a process that is fast, visible, and fair.`

Click:

- `Next`

### Screen 3

Visible text:

- `03 · The Solution`
- `PROVA verifies the beneficiary before the payout is approved.`

Say:

`This is our solution.`

`PROVA verifies first, makes a clear decision, and only then allows payout.`

`So instead of paying first and checking later, trust comes before transfer.`

Click:

- `Next`

### Screen 4

Visible text:

- `04 · Squad Integration`
- `When a beneficiary is cleared, Squad handles the rest.`

Say:

`This is where Squad becomes central.`

`Once a beneficiary is cleared, Squad powers the payout rail.`

`In our system, Squad is not a side feature. It is the layer that moves the verified funds.`

Click:

- `Next`

### Screen 5

Visible text:

- `05 · AI Intelligence`
- `The trust decision is explainable, not a black box.`

Say:

`This is our intelligence layer.`

`The system combines verification signals and risk checks into one clear outcome: approved, review, or rejected.`

`And it explains why, using risk score and reason codes.`

Click:

- `Next`

### Screen 6

Visible text:

- `06 · Live Demo`
- `The full product is running right now.`

Say:

`Now we move from explanation into the live product.`

`We are about to show the real dashboard, the verification flow, and the payout rail.`

Click:

- `Enter app`
